Overview

This agreement covers the full website design, development, hosting, and ongoing maintenance provided for the Snacks & Sips website. This plan includes the complete initial build of the website as well as a flat monthly fee of $125 to keep the site live, updated, and professionally maintained. All requests are handled promptly at no additional cost.

Services Included

  • Full Website Design & Development — Complete custom design and build of the Snacks & Sips website, including all pages, content, imagery, and branding.
  • Website Hosting — Site remains live and accessible 24/7. All hosting and domain management handled by the service provider.
  • Content Updates — Changes to text, pricing, menu items, or any written content updated upon request.
  • Photo & Image Updates — New photos uploaded and placed on the site whenever needed.
  • Design & Layout Changes — Updates to the visual appearance, structure, or layout of any page.
  • New Sections or Offerings — Adding new menu items, services, or pages as the business grows.
  • Bug Fixes & Error Resolution — Anything that breaks is fixed the same day at no additional charge.
  • Mobile & Device Compatibility — Site displays correctly on phones, tablets, and desktops at all times.
  • Seasonal & Promotional Updates — Updates for holidays, special events, promotions, or seasonal changes.
  • Priority Support — All requests are given priority response as an active monthly client.

Pricing

Monthly Rate: $125.00 — flat rate, all services included

No hourly fees, no per-update charges, no surprise costs. Everything above is covered regardless of how many updates are requested.

Terms & Conditions

Billing Invoiced monthly. Payment is due at the start of each billing cycle.
Minimum Commitment This agreement requires a minimum commitment of 12 to 24 months from the start date, as agreed upon by both parties. The client may not cancel before the agreed term is completed.
Cancellation After the agreed minimum term, either party may cancel with 30 days written notice. No long-term contract required beyond the initial term.
